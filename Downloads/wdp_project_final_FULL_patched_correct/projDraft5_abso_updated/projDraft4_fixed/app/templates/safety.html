Safety & Trust

Your safety comes first on Re:Connect SG.

Emergency: If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, call 999 (Police), 995 (Ambulance/SCDF), or reach out to someone you trust right away.

1. Keep Your Identity Secure

  • Do not share NRIC numbers, bank details, or passwords in chats.
  • Use a strong password and keep it private.
  • Report any suspicious messages or requests immediately.

2. Meeting in Person

  • Meet in public, well-lit locations with people around.
  • Let a friend or family member know where you are going.
  • Trust your instincts and leave if anything feels off.

3. Community Standards

  • Be respectful and kind in all interactions.
  • No harassment, bullying, or discrimination.
  • No requests for money, gifts, or personal financial details.

4. Trust Score System

Re:Connect SG uses a transparent Safety Score to keep the community safe. Your score is private and helps determine access to higher-trust features.

  • How it works: Scores start at 50 and move based on behavior and participation.
  • Earn points: Join verified circles, complete safe meetups, and maintain a clean record.
  • If your score drops: Some features may be limited until trust is restored.

Want to see your score? Visit your profile: Safety & Trust.

5. Reporting & Blocking

Use the in-app report and block features if you encounter unsafe behaviour. Reports help us keep the community safe and are reviewed by the admin team.
